Diabetes Mellitus

Metabolic Disorder

üìä Select Age Group for Specific Information:

üìã Overview

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar (glucose). When you have diabetes, your body either doesn't make enough insulin or can't effectively use the insulin it produces.

üîç Common Symptoms

Early Signs

Increased thirst, frequent urination, extreme hunger, unexplained weight loss

Progressive Symptoms

Fatigue, blurred vision, slow-healing sores, frequent infections

Advanced Signs

Numbness in extremities, darkened skin areas, recurrent yeast infections

üë§ Age-Specific Guidance (Adults)

Risk Factors: Family history, obesity, physical inactivity, age over 45

Management: Regular blood sugar monitoring, balanced diet, exercise routine, medication adherence

Complications: Heart disease, kidney damage, nerve damage, eye problems
